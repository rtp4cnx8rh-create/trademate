Whether you have a question about LiveSync setup or analytics.

How do I set up LiveSync?

Go to the "Broker Integration" section in settings and follow the instructions to generate your API key at your broker. Add this key to TradeMate. Synchronization will begin immediately.

Which brokers are supported?

We currently support all major platforms such as Interactive Brokers, TradeStation, DeGiro and most MetaTrader-based brokers. You can find a complete list in the app under "Integration".

How can I contact TradeMate?

Send us an email at support@trademate.de or use our contact form in the app. Pro and Ultimate customers also have access to our live chat.
